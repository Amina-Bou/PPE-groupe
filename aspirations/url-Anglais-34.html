Tube strikes this weekend - check your travel

25 November 2021

If you are visiting one of our hospitals this weekend, please check your travel and plan ahead.

Transport for London (TFL) has planned a 24 hour tube strike on five lines starting early morning on 26 November.

Another will begin in the evening on Saturday 27 November at 8.30pm until 4.29am, Sunday 28 November. These will be on the central and victoria lines.

You can find more information on TFL's website.
